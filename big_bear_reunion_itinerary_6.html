Cabin breakfast + optional: Alpine Pedal Path morning walk
Simple spread at the hub cabin first — fruit, pastries, bagels, eggs. Then, for anyone who wants it, the Alpine Pedal Path is a free, fully paved 3.2-mile trail along the north shore of Big Bear Lake. Completely flat with stunning lake and mountain views. Perfect for grandparents, toddlers in strollers, and the whole group together. Takes about 1.5 hrs at a relaxed pace — just show up and walk.

Big cabin breakfast + optional: Big Bear Alpine Zoo
Frittatas, fresh fruit, pastries — the cooks take the morning. For families with young kids and grandparents who want a gentler start before the afternoon splits, the Big Bear Alpine Zoo is a wonderful 1–2 hour detour on the way to activities. It's a wildlife rehabilitation center housing grizzly bears, snow leopards, mountain lions, bald eagles, wolves, and more. Small, unhurried, and meaningful — all animals are rescued and can't return to the wild. Wheelchair accessible with ADA parking.
✅ ADA accessible perfect for toddlers & grandparents
